⚠️ Mac — One extra step required

macOS may show "App is damaged" — this is a security prompt for apps not on the App Store. FrontStores is completely safe.

Fix in 10 seconds:
1. Copy the app to Applications folder
2. Open Terminal (Spotlight → type Terminal)
3. Paste this command and press Enter:
xattr -cr /Applications/FrontStores.app
Then double-click the app — opens normally. One-time only.

ℹ️ Windows — SmartScreen popup

Windows may show "Windows protected your PC" — this is normal for new apps not yet widely distributed. FrontStores is safe.

Click More info → then Run anyway to install.

FAQ

Common questions

How does the free trial work? ▾
Download and install the app — no credit card needed. You get full access to all features for 30 days completely free. After 30 days, a subscription of ₹999/month is required to continue.
Does it work without internet? ▾
Yes, completely. FrontStores runs 100% offline. All data is on your computer. You can bill customers, manage inventory and view reports with no internet. Internet is only needed for software updates and subscription verification (once every 7 days).
Is my data safe? What if I uninstall? ▾
Your data is stored only on your computer — nothing is ever uploaded to a cloud server. If you uninstall or cancel, your data remains on your machine and is never deleted remotely. You can always export a full backup from Settings.
How do I pay after the trial? ▾
Use the contact form on this page. We'll extend your subscription manually within a few hours. No payment gateway, no auto-debit — you decide when to renew.
Can I use it on multiple computers? ▾
One subscription is for one shop installation on one computer. Multiple computers or shops each need a separate subscription. Multi-device sync is on the roadmap.
Which Windows and Mac versions are supported? ▾
Windows 10 and Windows 11 (64-bit). For Mac: macOS 12 Monterey and above, both Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) and Intel. Windows 7 and 8 are not supported.
