⚠️ Mac — One extra step required

macOS may show "App is damaged" — this is a security prompt for apps not on the App Store. FrontStores is safe.

Fix in 10 seconds: Copy the app to Applications, open Terminal, paste this and press Enter:
xattr -cr /Applications/FrontStores.app
Then double-click — opens normally. One time only.

ℹ️ Windows — SmartScreen popup

Windows may show "Windows protected your PC" — normal for new apps. FrontStores is safe.

Click More info → then Run anyway to install.

ℹ️ Android — Install from outside Play Store

Android may show "Install blocked for your safety" — normal for apps installed outside the Play Store. FrontStores is safe.

Tap Settings in that prompt → enable "Allow from this source" → go back and tap Install.
