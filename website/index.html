⚠️ Mac Installation — One extra step required

macOS may show "App is damaged" when you first open it. This is a security warning for apps not sold on the App Store — FrontStores is safe.

To fix it:
1. Copy the app to your Applications folder
2. Open Terminal (search in Spotlight)
3. Paste this command and press Enter:
xattr -cr /Applications/FrontStores.app

Then double-click the app — it will open normally. You only need to do this once.

ℹ️ Windows Installation Note

Windows SmartScreen may show "Windows protected your PC". This is normal for new apps not yet widely distributed.

Click More info → then Run anyway to install.

FAQ

Common questions

How does the free trial work? ▾
Download and install the app — no credit card needed. You get full access to all features for 30 days completely free. After 30 days, a subscription of ₹999/month is required to continue using the app.
Does it work without internet? ▾
Yes. FrontStores runs completely offline. All data is stored on your computer. You can bill customers, manage inventory, and view reports with no internet. Internet is only used for software updates and subscription verification.
What happens to my data if I cancel? ▾
Your data stays on your computer forever — it is never deleted. If you cancel your subscription, the app stops accepting new bills but you can still view all your old records and reports.
Can I use it on multiple computers? ▾
One subscription covers one shop installation. If you have multiple computers or shops, each needs a separate subscription. Multi-device sync is on the roadmap as a future feature.
Is GST filing supported? ▾
FrontStores generates GSTR-ready sales summaries with CGST, SGST, and taxable value breakdowns by period. Use these to fill your GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B manually. Direct e-filing integration is planned.
Which Windows versions are supported? ▾
Windows 10 and Windows 11, 64-bit. Windows 7 and 8 are not supported. For Mac: macOS 12 (Monterey) and above, both Intel and Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4).
