FETCHING LATEST VERSION…
Mac — one extra step. macOS may show "App is damaged" — a standard prompt for apps outside the App Store. FrontStores is safe. Copy the app to Applications, open Terminal, paste this and press Enter:
xattr -cr /Applications/FrontStores.app
Then open it normally. One time only.
Windows — SmartScreen popup. Windows may show "Windows protected your PC" — normal for new apps. FrontStores is safe. Click More info → Run anyway.
Android — outside Play Store. Android may show "Install blocked for your safety" — normal for direct downloads. FrontStores is safe. Tap Settings → enable "Allow from this source" → tap Install.
