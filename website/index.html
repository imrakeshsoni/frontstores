FAQ

Questions, answered

Where is my data stored — and is it safe?

By default your data is stored securely on the cloud, encrypted and backed up automatically, so you can use it from any device. Prefer total privacy? We offer a local-only mode where your data stays on your computer and never goes to the cloud.

Can I use it on more than one device?

Yes. Because your data is on the cloud, you can log in from your shop computer, home laptop or phone and everything stays in sync automatically.

Can my whole team use it?

Yes — you can create multiple users with different roles (owner, cashier, manager) for your business. Just ask and we'll set it up.

Is it GST-ready?

Yes — CGST/SGST invoices, HSN codes and Bill of Supply for non-GST shops, with reports your CA will love.

What if I need a feature you don't have?

We build custom features and even fully custom software. Tell us your workflow and we'll tailor it to fit your business.

How much does it cost?

₹999/month flat, with a 30-day free trial — no card needed. Cloud is included by default; extra users, local-only mode and custom builds are quoted separately.
