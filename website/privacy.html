Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

FrontStores is built privacy-first. This policy explains what information we handle and how. By using the Software, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Where Your Business Data Is Stored

By default, your business data — products, customers, orders, billing, inventory, and similar records — is stored securely on the cloud and synced across your devices so you can access it from anywhere. You remain the owner of your data at all times. If you choose our local-only mode, your business data is kept solely on your own device and is not uploaded to the cloud.

2. Information We Do Collect

To register your account, deliver updates, and manage your subscription, we collect limited information:

  • Registration details — shop name, city, and the mobile number (WhatsApp number) you provide at signup, used to identify your installation and contact you.
  • Subscription & licence status — your tenant identifier, plan, and expiry date, used to validate your subscription.
  • Error reports — if the app encounters an error, technical diagnostic details may be sent to help us fix issues. These reports are intended to contain technical information, not your business records.

3. How We Use This Information

  • To create and manage your account and subscription.
  • To verify your licence and deliver software updates.
  • To contact you about your subscription, support, or important notices.
  • To diagnose and fix technical problems.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your information. We do not share it with third parties except as required to operate the service (for example, infrastructure providers that host our update and licensing servers) or as required by law.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we hold, including encryption of data stored on the cloud and regular automatic backups. Your account security also depends on you keeping your login credentials and devices secure. For local-only installations, data security and backups are your responsibility.

6. Data Retention

We retain registration and subscription information for as long as your account is active and as needed to comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your registration information by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the registration information we hold about you. You can manage, export, or delete your business data at any time from within the app; for cloud accounts you may also request export or deletion of your data by contacting us.

8. Children

FrontStores is intended for business use and is not directed to individuals under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Software after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, reach out via the contact form on our home page.
